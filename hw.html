My Favourite Hobby

Drawing and Painting

Drawing and painting are fundamental visual arts, with drawing focusing on line, structure, and tone using dry media (pencil, charcoal), while painting emphasizes color and texture through wet media (oil, acrylic, watercolor) on surfaces like canvas.



My Hobby

Playing

Not only does play promote and aid in physical development (such as hand-eye coordination), but it also aids in cognitive development and social skills, and can even act as a stepping stone into the world of integration, which can be a very stressful process.

My Hobby

Reading Story Books

Reading storybooks enhances cognitive development, vocabulary, and empathy in both children and adults, while reducing stress. It boosts imagination, strengthens brain function, and improves focus. Regular reading fosters better communication skills, critical thinking, and provides emotional comfort.
